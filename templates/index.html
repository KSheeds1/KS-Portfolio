About Me

Hello world, my name is Katie. I'm a recently qualified Full Stack Software Developer living in Ireland. I'm currently available for work and am also open to moving! I received my diploma in Full Stack Software Development with Code Institute, the only university accredited bootcamp in Europe. I have a complimentary background in hospitality and TEFL and have always enjoyed working as part of a team.

Prior to the pandemic, I left my position as a bar and restaurant supervisor and backpacked around eastern Europe. I've had a curiosty for technology and the 'behind the scenes' of the internet from a young age, so when I returned to Ireland, I decided to finally take the plunge and began learning to code. I throughly enjoyed my time with Code Institute and have learned so much. I am eagerly pursuing a career in software development so please do get in contact!
